Our mission

In a world dominated by a few big players with expensive and complicated offerings, we want to shake things up. We believe that everyone should have access to the same tools and services, regardless of their size or budget. That's why we created Elite Sentry, a simple and affordable monitoring solution for websites and APIs.

Whether you are a startup/SMB owner with one or two websites or a SRE/DevOps with tons of websites and APIs to monitor, we have something for you. Our goal is to make it easy for you to know if the things you care about are working as expected, so you can focus on what really matters.

SMBs and startups

Working for a startups, small, or medium business? You will appreciate the ease of use and the affordable pricing of Elite Sentry. Our monitoring solution is designed to help you monitor you website(s) without getting in the way. You can set up monitoring in minutes and get notified when something goes wrong.

DevOps, Sysadmins, and SREs

Don't let our ease of use and affordable pricing fool you. Elite Sentry is a powerful monitoring solution that can scale with your needs. Advanced features let you customize request headers, define the expected response, configure downtimes, integrate with third parties, and much more so that you can monitor your APIs and websites the way you want.
