Can I stay on the standard tier at no cost forever?

Yes, absolutely! You can stay on the standard tier at no cost forever. However, we encourage users to contribute through a 'pay-what-you-want' model to help us continue delivering value to everyone.

I need more, do I really need to change to a bigger plan?

Not necessarily. We understand how frustrating it can be when vendors force users to upgrade to a much more expensive plan for just one additional feature. That’s why we’re working on offering 'add-ons,' so you can easily get more monitors or longer data retention without switching to a bigger plan.
